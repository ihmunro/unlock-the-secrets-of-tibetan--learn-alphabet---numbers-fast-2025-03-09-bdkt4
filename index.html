Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Tibetan language learning platform.

How long does it take to learn the Tibetan alphabet?

With our structured approach, most students can recognize and write all 30 basic Tibetan consonants within 2-3 weeks of consistent practice. The vowel marks and combined characters typically take an additional 2-4 weeks to master.

Do I need any prior language learning experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our platform is designed for complete beginners. The interactive lessons gradually introduce concepts, ensuring a smooth learning experience regardless of your background.

Can I learn Tibetan numbers without learning the alphabet?

Absolutely! Our course is modular, allowing you to focus on specific areas of interest. The number system module stands alone and can be completed independently of the alphabet lessons.

Is there support if I get stuck on a particular concept?

Yes, we offer multiple support options. You'll have access to our community forum, detailed explanations with each lesson, and optional 1-on-1 tutoring sessions with native Tibetan speakers for personalized guidance.

Our Story

About Our Learning Platform

Learn more about our mission and the team behind TibetanLearning.

Our Tibetan language learning platform was founded in 2021 by a team of passionate linguists and Tibetan culture enthusiasts. We recognized that while there are many resources for popular languages, the beautiful and historically significant Tibetan language had limited accessible learning materials.

Our mission is to preserve and promote Tibetan language and culture through innovative digital education. We work with native speakers from different regions of Tibet to ensure authenticity in pronunciation and usage.

What sets our approach apart is our deep integration of cultural context with language learning. As you progress through our lessons, you'll gain insights into Tibetan history, philosophy, and daily life, making your learning journey more meaningful and engaging.

Whether you're a traveler planning a trip to Tibet, a scholar interested in accessing original Buddhist texts, or simply a language enthusiast looking for a new challenge, our platform provides the tools and community to support your goals.
